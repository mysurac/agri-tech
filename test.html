Rotavator is a versatile tractor implement to perform different soil functions such as mixing, pulverization, puddling & leveling at the same time.Fieldking Rotavator is very useful for removing the past crop residues from the field and preparing the soil for the next crop. It breaks up the hardpan layers of the soil and makes it levelled for sowing. Its strong frame assembly helps the implement to work in different types of soil without any wear and tear. It loosens and aerates soil up to 7 inches deep.

Key Features of Rotavator

  • Blades used in Fieldking Rotavator are of Boron steel which lasts 50% more than Hi Carbon.
  • Helical arrangement of the blades puts less load on the tractor which makes tillage fast and economical.
