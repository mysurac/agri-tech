Product Portfolio

Rotavator

Rotavators are agricultural tools used to prepare soil by breaking it up, mixing in organic matter, controlling weeds, ultimately reducing manual labor and improving crop yields

Harvester

Harvesters are agricultural machines designed to efficiently gather mature crops from fields, streamlining the process of crop collection and increasing agricultural productivity.

Cultivator

Cultivators are agricultural implements used to mechanically loosen and aerate soil, remove weeds, and prepare seedbeds, enhancing soil quality and promoting optimal conditions for crop growth.

Ploughs

Plows are essential tools in agriculture used for primary tillage, breaking up soil, burying crop residues, and preparing the ground for planting, promoting soil aeration and nutrient distribution while controlling weeds.

Trailers

Trailers in agriculture are transportation units designed to haul various materials such as harvested crops, livestock, machinery, and supplies, facilitating efficient movement within farms and between different agricultural sites.

Balers

Balers in agriculture are machines utilized to compact and bundle harvested crops such as hay, straw, or silage into manageable and transportable packages, optimizing storage space and facilitating ease of handling and transportation.
